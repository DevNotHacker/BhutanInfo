Why

There is a big issue in Bhutan. Bhutan has a very high poverty rate, and I want to fix that. Zero Poverty is the first goal in global goals, which you can check out at this website.

It is one thing to think of ideas, like making a website, but it is another to actually do the idea, and in this example, actually make this website. I wanted to be one person who actually did my idea, and try to change the world. If this problem is not solved, children and adults will be in poverty, and no one deserves to be in poverty. Most people in bhutan who are in poverty are just unfortunate, like having a flood destroy their home.

I can make a website, so I decided to make a website, to spread awareness. There are many people who know the global goals, and some that try to help contribute to achieving the global goals. There is not enough, so I wanted to spread awareness, and hopefully, there will not be that much poverty in Bhutan.
