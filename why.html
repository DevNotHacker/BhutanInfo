Why is there Poverty?

Natural Disasters happen frequently, because of the Himalayan landscape. Floods destroy villages and crops.

Some people are in poverty because there is lack of education and/or a lack of jobs. A lot of people in rural areas of Bhutan have many family members, which make it hard to take care of. Also, people in rural areas have to walk 2-3 hours to get to the nearest school.
